
# Object Oriented Design Patterns for Software

- Foundational concepts for object oriented programming - **Abstraction**, **Polymorphism**, **Inheritence**, **Encapsulation** (***A-P-I-E***)
- Design patterns **complement** A-P-I-E, bridging the gap between basic OOP concepts and complex challenges. 
- Built on `SOLID` practices, these patterns are tried and tested strategies, shared and evolved by the developer community.
    > While constructing a building, understanding the basics like bricks, cement, and steel (A-P-I-E in our context) is crucial.

    > But, it's the architectural blueprints and patterns that ensure the building stands tall, is functional, aesthetic, and safe.
- While A-P-I-E sets the groundwork, design patterns are those architectural blueprints for software, ensuring it’s resilient, scalable, and maintainable.

### Types of design patterns
- Creational
- Structural
- Behavioral
